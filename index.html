Hi, I'm Amy.

I've always been able to feel a space.

From wine bar, to hotel lobby, to event auditorium — every space tells a story. But the experience your guests have isn't just what they see. It's how your team makes them feel, the music in the background, the moment someone looks up and actually acknowledges them.

I've always been able to walk into a room and sense what's working and what isn't — across every layer of the experience. After a decade producing major events, tailoring guest experiences, and working with luxury brands and businesses, I realised that the most memorable properties aren't just beautifully designed. They're intentionally felt.

The gap between a good experience and an unforgettable one is smaller than most properties realise.

If you're looking to elevate your business and enhance your guest experience, let's have a conversation.
